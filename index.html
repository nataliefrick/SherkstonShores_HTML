About

Sherkston Shores is the right choice for all - who are searching for the best place for fun with the family.

The cottage is located in the Sherkston Shores Beach Resort which is an all ages resort for fun in the sun. Searching for a kid-friendly vacation spot? You have found it! Book our cottage for a week of fun and family time with a combination of charm and a convenient position from where to explore surroundings.
